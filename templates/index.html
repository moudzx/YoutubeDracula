YouTube Dracula

Sunk in fangs, drained the audio. Paste a link, take the whole victim, just the blood, or one bitten slice.

Only use this on videos you have the rights to download.

What the count can do

  • Single video downloads — paste any video URL and grab it as a video or as audio-only, at whatever quality the source offers.
  • Section trimming — drag the range sliders or type exact start/end times to drain out just that section instead of the whole body.
  • MP3 conversion — convert audio-only downloads straight to MP3 (192kbps) with one checkbox.
  • Playlist support — paste a playlist URL to see every track listed with its duration before you download anything.
  • Playlist — one combined file — drain an entire playlist into a single audio file (MP3, M4A, Opus, or WAV) or a single bled-together video (MP4), in track order.
  • Playlist — separate files — or keep every track as its own victim — audio or video — delivered together in one .zip.
  • Quality control — choose exact video resolutions (up to 1080p and beyond) and audio bitrates/formats before downloading.
